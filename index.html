Hello there

Hi! hello, I am a Master's student at University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) , specializing in Biomedical Imaging technologies. Before that, I worked as a Post-Baccalaureate researcher at Indian Institute of Technology Madras, India in the field of Computational Neuroscience in BCI to understand the underlying neural mechanisms of BCI control. Subsequently, as a Visiting Summer researcher at Paris Brain Institute, I worked on formulating interpretable deep learning tools for BCI applications, where I implemented spatial filtering methods to better classify the left and right motor imagery tasks.


I hold a Bachelor's degree in Biotechnology from National Institute of Technology Durgapur, India. There, as an undergraduate researcher I worked on Drug Delivery systems.


My primary research interest lies in uncovering the conundrum behind brain dynamics🧠 through computational approaches and advanced imaging modalities.
